Garlic Toast Recipe

Great garlic toast with no added fat! My family asks for this all the time!

Prep: 10 mins

Cook: 5 mins

Total: 15 mins

Servings: 4

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

Â½ (1 pound) loaf French baguette, cut into 1/2 inch slices

6 cloves garlic, halved

Steps

Step 1: Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler. Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet.

Step 2: Toast in preheated oven until golden brown, 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 3: Rub garlic halves on warm bread.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 171 calories; protein 7g; carbohydrates 33.5g; fat 1.1g; sodium 369.6mg.
